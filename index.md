# Evenfall

**Soft on the eyes.** Evenfall is a Mac menu-bar app that gently dims bright windows and
adds a warm glow, so living in dark mode no longer means getting jolted by a white page.
It works automatically, across every screen — no fiddling with brightness.

## Common questions

**Why does Evenfall ask for Screen Recording permission?**
It measures the *brightness* of what's on screen so it knows how much to dim — that's all.
Evenfall never records, screenshots, stores, or sends any of your screen content. The
measurement happens on your Mac and stays there.

**Does the dimming show up in screen recordings or video calls?**
No. The dimming overlay is excluded from capture, so Zoom, QuickTime and the like see your
screen at full brightness.

**What's free, and what's Pro?**
The eye-break reminders are free forever. All the dimming features — warmth, presets, the
Dark Room glow, the night schedule and multi-display — are **Evenfall Pro**. You can start
with a free trial on the yearly plan, or buy Lifetime once.

**How do I manage or cancel a subscription?**
On your Mac:  → System Settings → your Apple ID → **Media & Purchases** → Subscriptions.
A Lifetime purchase isn't a subscription — there's nothing to cancel.

**How do I restore a purchase on a new Mac?**
Open Evenfall's unlock screen and tap **Restore purchase**.
