# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 15 June 2026**

Evenfall is built to respect your privacy completely. The short version: **Evenfall does not
collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** There is no account, no analytics, and no
tracking.

## What Evenfall does *not* do

- It does **not** create an account or ask for any personal information.
- It does **not** use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
- It does **not** send your data anywhere. Evenfall has no servers.

## Screen Recording permission

Evenfall asks macOS for the **Screen Recording** permission so it can measure how *bright*
your screen is and decide how much to dim. This measurement happens entirely on your Mac.

- Evenfall reads only an **average brightness value** from the screen.
- It **never** records, screenshots, saves, or transmits any of your screen content.
- The dimming overlay is excluded from capture, so it doesn't appear in your own recordings
  or video calls.

## Settings stored on your device

Your preferences (dim level, warmth, presets, schedule, break settings, and so on) are saved
**locally** on your Mac using the standard macOS preferences system. They never leave your
device and are removed if you delete the app.

## Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are handled by **Apple** through the App Store using Apple's
in-app purchase system. Evenfall never sees your payment details. Apple's handling of that
information is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Children

Evenfall is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
